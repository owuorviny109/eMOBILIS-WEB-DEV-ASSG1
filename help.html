Frequently asked questions

How do I transition my cat to Bonkers?

Gradually mix Bonkers with your current food over 7-10 days, increasing the ratio each day.

Is Bonkers suitable for kittens?

Yes! Our formula meets AAFCO standards for all life stages, from kittens to seniors.

Where is Bonkers made?

All our food is made in small batches in Wisconsin, USA with locally sourced ingredients.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Absolutely! If your cat doesn't love it, we'll refund your purchase within 30 days.
